Applications close 31 July 2026

The Singapore cohort begins 1 August and runs through to commissioning in May 2027. The application itself takes about fifteen minutes, on the Colson Center's official portal.

Ready to apply

Start your application

You'll apply directly through the Colson Center's official application system — the same one every cohort worldwide uses. Please mention Singapore and the fortnightly Thursday-evening cohort when asked about your preferred format.

Apply on colsonfellows.org →

Have questions first?

Talk to the Singapore team

Carol Loi directs the Singapore cohort. If you're weighing this up, or want to confirm cost, dates, or anything else before you commit, she's the right person to ask.

Email cloi@colsonfellows.org

Before you go

Applying isn't a binding commitment on its own — it starts a conversation with the Colson Center and the Singapore team about whether this cohort, and this season, are the right fit. There's no pressure to have it all figured out first.
